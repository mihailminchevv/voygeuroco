Voyageur & Co is a travel-focused startup that reimagines how people discover cities in Europe. Instead of static guides and generic recommendations, we build structured, intelligent travel experiences powered by curated data and AI-based planning.

Our platform combines interactive maps, detailed city directories, and an AI trip planner that adapts to your travel style, duration, and interests. Whether you're exploring Berlin’s Cold War history, Parisian architecture, or Rome’s ancient landmarks — we help you experience each city in depth, not just as a tourist.

The goal is simple: replace overwhelming travel planning with clarity, structure, and discovery.
