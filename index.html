Featured Places

Must-See in Varna

Tourist Place

Cathedral of the Assumption

One of Bulgaria's largest cathedrals, consecrated in 1886. Its soaring neo-Byzantine domes and spectacular frescoes make it Varna's most iconic landmark.

Tourist Place

The Sea Garden

An 80-hectare coastal park along the Black Sea, home to the Dolphinarium, Summer Theatre, and miles of breathtaking clifftop walkways.

Tourist Place

Roman Thermae

The largest ancient Roman baths on the Balkan Peninsula. Dating from the 2nd century AD, the ruins stand nearly 20 metres high and are unforgettable at dusk.
