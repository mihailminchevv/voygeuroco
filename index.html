Featured Places

Must-See in Berlin

Strategic Legacy

The Reichstag

The seat of the German Parliament. An architectural masterpiece of stone and glass, representing the turbulent evolution of European power and democratic resilience.

Cold War Frontier

Checkpoint Charlie

The most famous crossing point between East and West. A silent witness to the intelligence battles of the Cold War and the ultimate symbol of a divided world.

Prussian Grandeur

Brandenburg Gate

The ultimate icon of Berlin. A neoclassical masterpiece commissioned by King Frederick William II, standing as a testament to Prussian glory and reunification.
